Lineup

Get ready to be blown away by our incredible lineup of artists, featuring some of the biggest names in music. From chart-topping hits to underground gems, our diverse selection caters to all tastes. Prepare for an unforgettable experience as you witness performances from:

Drake: The award-winning rapper and singer-songwriter will deliver his signature energy and unforgettable hits.

Dua Lipa: The global pop sensation will take the stage with her infectious beats and powerful vocals.

Bruno Mars: The multi-talented artist will bring his electrifying stage presence and soulful music.

Sabrina Carpenter: The rising star will showcase her incredible voice and captivating performances.

Kendrick Lamar: The critically acclaimed rapper and songwriter will deliver a thought-provoking and powerful set.

John Mayer: The legendary guitarist and singer will mesmerize the crowd with his soulful melodies.

Taylor Swift: The pop icon will perform her chart-topping hits and timeless classics.

And much more! Stay tuned for additional announcements as we reveal more of our exciting lineup.
